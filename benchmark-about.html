About Benchmark Financial

Built on Trust.
Driven by Purpose.

We are a family office built on the belief that every family deserves an advisor who is legally, ethically, and personally committed to their best interest — without exception.

Our Story

We started Benchmark Financial because we believed families deserved better. Better advice. Better transparency. A firm that would treat their wealth and their trust with the seriousness it deserves.

For over two decades, we have served high-net-worth families across South Florida and beyond as a true family office, providing the kind of comprehensive, coordinated financial oversight that was once reserved only for the ultra-wealthy. As fee-only fiduciaries, we earn no commissions and accept no referral fees. We are paid by our clients, and only our clients, to give advice that is completely and unconditionally in their best interest. That commitment has never wavered. It is the foundation of everything we do and everyone we serve.
